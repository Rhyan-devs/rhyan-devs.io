Welcome to the world of PABLO

The Lone Wolf

Pablo (JOHN PAULO BAGNAS NASE) is a Filipino singer, song-writer, rapper, and producer who debuted as a solo artist on the 28th of January 2022, with his track "La Luna".

He is also known as the main songwriter, lyricist, producer, and leader of the Filipino Pop group SB19, and the CEO of 1Z Entertainment, a company established and managed by SB19 members.

PABLO is also part of a duo called RADKIDZ, a collaboration between him and his brother Josue. They recently released two songs titled “Akala” and “Determinado” in which the latter song was hailed grand winner in the DJOOKY Music Awards Spring Edition back in 2021.
